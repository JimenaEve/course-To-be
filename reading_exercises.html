Reading exercises

Reading comprehension

This interactive presentation allows students to learn the verb "to be" in a visual and structured way. Through clear examples like "I am a student," "She is happy," and "They are friends.

1. Identify the grammatical forms (am/is/are) based on the subject.
2. Practice sentence construction (affirmative, negative, and interrogative forms).
3. Connect the verb to images and everyday contexts
