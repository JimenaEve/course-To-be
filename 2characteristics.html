2. Characteristics

Characteristics

- It is an irregular verb. - It changes form according to the pronoun (I am, you are, he/she/it is, etc.).

- It can be used as a main verb and also as an auxiliary.

- It is the basis for forming tenses such as the present continuous and structures such as the passive voice.
