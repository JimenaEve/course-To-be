Writing exercises

Complete the text with the verb to be

Writing short paragraphs with the verb "to be" is a great writing activity for children. It helps them identify forms like am, is, and are, and use them correctly. These texts use real-life situations, like family or pets, making learning easier, more meaningful, and fun. They also improve vocabulary, reading comprehension, and confidence to write and understand English.
